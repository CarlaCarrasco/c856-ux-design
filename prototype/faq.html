Frequently Asked Questions

Here are most of our frequently asked questions. If you have a question that is not listed below, please email us at questions@tanitixyz.com.

Question: What is the power outage voltage rate?

Answer: 120 volts

Question: What is the drinking age?

Answer: The drinking age in Taniti is 18 years old, but this is not strictly enforced.

Question: Do the locals speak english?

Answer: Older Tanitians and locals in rural areas speak very little English. Many youger Tanitians residents speak are fluent in English.

Question: Is there a hospital on the island?

Answer: Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. Many hospital employees are multilingual.

Question: What is the crime scene like in Taniti?

Answer: As tourism increases, we have seen a spike in petty crimes and pickpoketing. Rarely does Taniti have violent crime.

Question: Do restaurants and attractions close during holidays?

Answer: Yes, Taniti has many national holidays and many restaurants and attractions will be closed during this time. You should plan accordingly during any Tanitian holiday.

Question: What currency is accepted in Taniti?

Answer: U.S. dollars are the main currency, but eruos and yen can be accepted at many business. Most banks offer currency exchanges and major credit cards are widely accepted.
